---
title: Project Delays
description: How to address missed deadlines or slow progress.
---

## Symptoms

- Milestones not met on schedule
- Communication slowed or unclear

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check updates">
    Review your shared project board in Notion or Figma for status and comments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact your PM">
    Email or message your Tonik project manager to clarify blockers and next steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule alignment call">
    Book a quick call with the Tonik team to realign priorities and adjust timeline if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
