## Prerequisites

Before contacting Tonik, prepare the following to ensure a smooth consultation:

<Callout kind="info">
- A clear description of your product vision and target audience
- Key challenges you're facing (e.g., UX/UI design, branding, or no-code implementation)
- Your timeline and budget range
- Status as a YC startup, VC portfolio company, or non-profit for special deals
</Callout>

Having these ready helps Tonik provide tailored advice quickly.

## Partnership Onboarding Process

Follow these steps to go from consultation to active partnership:

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Initial Consultation" icon="phone">
    Join the `{30-60}` minute discovery call. Discuss your goals, share your prepared materials, and receive initial feedback.
  </Step>
  <Step title="2. Outline Project Needs" icon="edit-3">
    Submit a detailed project brief covering scope, milestones, and success metrics.

    Example brief structure:

````markdown
# Project Brief: [Project Name]

## Goals
- Improve user retention by `{20%}`

## Scope
- Wireframes and prototypes
- Full UI implementation

## Timeline
- Kickoff: [Date]
- Delivery: [Date]
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="3. Proposal and Agreement" icon="file-text">
    Review Tonik's customized proposal, including pricing and timeline. Sign the agreement to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="4. Onboarding and Kickoff" icon="rocket">
    Access shared tools (Figma, Notion), assign points of contact, and hold the kickoff meeting. Projects typically launch within `{1-2}` weeks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
